The Pattern Behind Every Transformation

1

Context & Constraints

Instead of vague requests, specify exactly what you need, who it's for, and what limitations exist.

2

Format & Structure

Tell AI exactly how to organize the output. Examples, templates, and formatting rules prevent rework.

3

Quality Standards

Define success criteria upfront. What makes this "good"? What tone, length, or style do you need?

4

Edge Cases & Boundaries

Specify what NOT to include and what to avoid. This prevents common AI mistakes and hallucinations.
